Statistics and Tips

Stats

  • 20% of people who participate in at least one social media platform have reported feeling anxious if they do not check on any updates within three hours.
  • 13% of individuals 12-17 years old have been reported as having depression.
  • 32% of individuals 12-17 years old have been reported as having depression.
  • 60% of adolescents use their phones within an hour of going to sleep, which can cause sleep deprivation.
  • 72% of teenagers have been cyberbullied.

Tips on How to Help Your Mental Health While Using Social Media

  • Set your phone to Do Not Disturb for social media notifications at certain times of the day.
  • Let time limits for yourself for certain apps that you notice are making you feel down or upset.
  • Remember that not all the content you see online is true or real, and that you should not compare yourself to others.
  • Try to use social media apps less before going to sleep.
